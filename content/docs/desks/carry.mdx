**Analytical desk** — promoted into the PM synthesis 2026-06-20 (previously a supporting input); not a
separate on-chain token. The Carry desk watches **delta-neutral funding-carry** — the yield from holding
spot and shorting the perp to harvest funding — a **market-neutral** opportunity, explicitly **not** a
directional signal.

## What it reads

`firm/organization.py · tool_carry()` (over `firm/carry_signal.py`):

- **This asset's carry** — trailing perp funding annualized vs the risk-free rate.
- **Best harvestable** — scans HYPE / SUI / MNT / BTC / ETH and returns the most attractive carry with its
  `crash_class` (crash-stress robustness) and verdict.

## Why it's only advisory

Funding-carry is a **yield** play, not alpha on price. It earns when funding is rich and the position is
held delta-neutral; **HYPE / SUI funding-carry is OOS-validated** (see [Findings](/docs/findings)), and it
is single-venue executable. But most of the time there is **no carry above risk-free**, so the desk simply
reports "nothing harvestable" and stays out of the directional vote.

## Output (shape — illustrative values)

```json
{
  "asset": "HYPE",
  "this_asset_carry": { "symbol": "HYPEUSDT", "carry_ann_pct": 0.0, "crash_class": "robust", "attractive": false },
  "best_harvestable": null,
  "read": "no harvestable carry above risk-free right now",
  "caveat": "market-neutral yield option, NOT a directional signal; HYPE/SUI funding-carry validated OOS"
}
```

## Honest caveats

Carry is **non-directional** — it never says "buy" or "sell" the asset; it flags a market-neutral yield
when one exists. It executes locally (Bybit / Hyperliquid), and it is quiet by design: most cycles report
no harvestable carry.
