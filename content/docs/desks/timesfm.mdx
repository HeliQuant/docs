**Analytical desk** — wired into the PM synthesis 2026-06-20; not yet a registered on-chain token. This
is the firm's **risk engine**: Google **TimesFM 2.5 (200M)**, a time-series foundation model, forecasting
**next-day realized volatility**. It is the one desk whose core output is **OOS-validated to beat the
gold standard** — and it is deliberately **not** a price-direction desk.

## The honest split: volatility YES, direction NO

We tested TimesFM both ways and published both results:

- **Volatility forecast — VALIDATED.** Next-day realized-vol beats **HAR-RV** (the econometric gold
  standard) and EWMA on BTC/ETH/SOL: **Diebold-Mariano p&lt;0.05, 3/3 walk-forward folds, two OOS
  windows** (`scripts/test_timesfm_vol_oos.py`). *(MNT was a tie with HAR — stated plainly.)*
- **Price direction — NO edge.** 24h-ahead direction is ~coin-flip and fee-eaten
  (`scripts/test_timesfm_oos.py`); covariate-fusion didn't help (`scripts/test_timesfm_covariates_oos.py`).
  So direction is **context-only** — never a vote.

## What it reads

`firm/timesfm_desk.py · tool_timesfm()`:

- **Realized-vol forecast (validated role)** — daily RV (√Σ hourly log-ret²) → TimesFM forecasts next-day
  RV → `regime` (rising / falling / stable), `percentile`, and a **vol-targeting `sizing_multiplier`**
  (target-vol ÷ forecast-vol, clipped 0.25–2.0).
- **Quantile band + abstain** — q10/q90 forecast band; if the band is too wide → `abstain_on_uncertainty`.
- **Regime-break anomaly** — compares a forecast made H bars ago against what actually happened; flags
  `regime_break` when the realized move lands outside the forecast band.

Backend self-reports `timesfm-2.5-200m` (local), `timesfm-remote` (HF Space), or `fallback-ewma-lognormal`.

- **Vol-targeting sizing** — `vol_sizing_factor()` feeds the trade-ticket sizer (`HQ_VOL_SIZING=1`): high
  forecast vol → size **down**, calm → size up (scoped to validated majors).
- **Risk-off stance** — `stance_guidance`: rising / high-percentile vol → **avoid** (de-risk); stable /
  low vol → **neutral**. It never votes bullish/bearish.
- **Anomaly alarm** — a `regime_break` warns the PM the tape just left its forecast envelope.

## Deployment

The 200M model is too heavy for the always-on Railway host, so it runs as a separate **Hugging Face Space**
microservice (free CPU, no geo-block) that the firm calls over HTTP (`TIMESFM_URL` / `TIMESFM_TOKEN`).
Unset → graceful EWMA fallback, so the firm never hard-depends on it.

## Honest caveats

The **volatility** forecast is the validated, risk-tradeable output (DM p&lt;0.05 on BTC/ETH/SOL). The
**price direction** has **no** edge and is shown only as context. This desk's job is to **size and
de-risk by forecast risk**, not to call price.
