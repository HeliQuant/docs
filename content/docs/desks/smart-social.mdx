**ERC-8004 tokenId 8.** The Smart-Social desk reads **where crypto attention is rotating** — using Elfa's
*smart-account* data (curated influential accounts, not retail noise). It quantifies **attention
magnitude**, not direction.

## What it reads

`firm/organization.py · tool_smartsocial()` → `firm/elfa_client.py`:

- **Trending narratives** — Elfa `trending-tokens` (24h mindshare): which tokens/narratives are heating up.
- **Per-asset top-mentions** — engagement-weighted mentions for the traded ticker.
- **Per-asset news** — recent token-news items.
- **Attention magnitude** — total views / mention count, e.g. **BTC ~1M vs a Mantle-eco asset ~3k
  views/24h** — a real, honest attention gap.

## Honest caveats

- **Attention is context, not direction.** A loud asset can dump; a quiet one can rip. The desk supplies
  *how much* an asset is in the conversation, and the PM decides what that's worth.
- **Elfa's AI trade-analysis chat (`/v2/chat`) is paid-tier** — our key returns `403 scope: chat`. So we
  use the **data** endpoints (which our key reaches) and encode Elfa's trading *framework* (no mid-range
  chop, dynamic-stall exit, event-risk) directly in the firm's [gates](/docs/architecture#the-discipline-gates).
- Mantle-eco social mindshare is structurally thin — the desk reports that honestly rather than inventing
  a narrative.
