**ERC-8004 tokenId 6.** The research desk is the firm's window onto the broader market mood. It pulls
**only free public APIs** (no paid walls) and bundles them into a context read for the PM. It's
deliberately cheap and extensible from the public-apis catalog.

## What it reads

`firm/organization.py · tool_research()`:

- **Fear & Greed index** — `alternative.me` (value + classification).
- **Global market** — CoinGecko: total market-cap 24h change, BTC dominance, active-crypto count.
- **Trending coins** — CoinGecko top trending symbols.
- **Majors 24h change** — CoinGecko simple-price for BTC / ETH / MNT.

## Honest caveats

This desk is **context, not signal** — sentiment indices and dominance are slow-moving and weakly
predictive at a 4h–24h horizon. Its job is to tell the PM what the *weather* is (risk-on vs risk-off),
not to call direction. Everything is keyless and free, so it never blocks a cycle on a quota.
