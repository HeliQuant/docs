Each desk runs a **real tool** over fresh data and returns a stance + confidence. Different desks run on
different LLM models so they don't groupthink. The firm synthesizes **ten specialist desks** plus two
self-learning inputs — **flow-intel** and **whale** — for **twelve analyst voices** into one PM decision.
**Every desk has its own deep-dive page** — linked in the table below.

> **On-chain vs analytical.** The firm + **9 desks** are registered ERC-8004 identities (tokenIds 2–11,
> [see On-chain](/docs/on-chain)). The three newest desks — **Carry, Mantle Fundamentals, TimesFM** — were
> wired into the PM synthesis on 2026-06-20 and run **analytical-only** (not yet minted as on-chain
> tokens). We say so rather than inflate the on-chain count.

## How the desks divide the problem

Twelve voices, three jobs: **predict** a pattern, **follow** informed actors, or supply **context / risk**.
The firm tilts which family leads based on each asset's measured character (see below).

## How they collaborate

The PM does **not** average the desks. Each desk earns a **reliability weight** from its own track
record; the PM ranks them, tags TRUSTED / DISCOUNT, runs a bull-vs-bear debate over the outputs, and only
then decides — behind a hard R:R gate.

## Which desks lead depends on the asset

`asset_efficiency.py` profiles each asset **fresh** every cycle (volatility + return-autocorrelation). A
**predictable** asset (autocorrelation ≥ 0.10, or a validated edge) boosts the **prediction** desks
(Regime, OI-Contrarian); an **efficient** asset (autocorrelation < 0.05, no exploitable pattern) boosts
the **flow** desks instead. Honest current read: **all majors are efficient right now** — even MNT's old
pattern has decayed — so flow desks lead and prediction desks are discounted.

## The ML is the Regime desk

We tested a directional ML (XGBoost BUY/SELL on MNT) — it scored **~51% accuracy and −50% to −62% OOS
net of fees** (Finding #22), so it is **not** used. The ML that *does* run is the **regime classifier**
(82.6% OOS, MNT-scoped, in **backtest**) inside the [Regime/Technical desk](/docs/desks/regime), used as a
**capital allocator / risk read**, not a price predictor. Live, the desk emits a cheap **rule-based regime
proxy** (`detect_current`); the XGBoost model is the backtest engine that earns the classifier its pedigree.
Honest ML: the good model earns its place; the disproven one stays out.

## Not a weighted desk: Character Tilt

**Character Tilt** — `asset_efficiency.py` profiles each asset (predictable vs flow-following) and tilts
the desk ranking (the quadrant above). It's a meta-weighting over the desks, not a voting desk.

## Reliability weights

The PM weights desks by **learned reliability** (`desk_performance`): each desk earns a weight
`0.6–1.4` from its realized track record (stance vs realized direction, ≥15 samples). Neutral until
proven, never muted. See [Self-learning](/docs/self-learning).
