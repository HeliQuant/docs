Honesty isn't a tagline here; it's the **design constraint**. Most "AI traders" optimize for a
screenshot. HeliQuant optimizes for a number you can reproduce.

## The rules we hold

## What "verifiable on Mantle" actually means

- **Identity** — 10 ERC-8004 agent tokens (firm + 9 desks) in the IdentityRegistry.
- **Sealed outputs** — each desk output hash written as a ValidationRegistry credential
  (`CredentialRecorded` event) per cycle.
- **Anchored trades** — each resolved trade's outcome SHA-256 in a transaction's calldata, recomputable
  by anyone from the public record.

Honest scope: these are **proof-of-existence hashes**, not capital moving. The paper floor trades at
live prices (Bybit testnet when armed); real capital stays gated behind a validated edge. We say so out
loud — *on the site, inside the product, and in the [findings](/docs/findings).*

## Why this is the moat

A magic indicator can be copied. **Rigor that retires its own flagship** can't be faked — and it's
exactly what a counterparty (or a hackathon judge) should trust. The discipline *is* the product.
