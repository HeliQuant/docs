## Honest scope

- **Decisions & outputs are sealed** (hash proof-of-existence) — verifiable, tamper-proof. Trades on
  the paper floor are **off-chain** (Bybit testnet when armed); the chain records the *decisions*, not
  every paper fill.
- **Reputation accrues forward** — it reads low until enough outcomes resolve. We don't pre-seed a
  flattering number.
- **The validation anchor is a snapshot**, not a forever-claim: when an edge later decays, the on-chain
  record stays as an immutable block-time fact, and the live registry demotes it.
