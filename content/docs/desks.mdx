different LLM models so they don't groupthink. All nine are registered ERC-8004 agents
([see On-chain](/docs/on-chain)).

## The ML is the Regime desk

We tested a directional ML (XGBoost BUY/SELL on MNT) — it scored **~51% accuracy and −50% to −62% OOS
net of fees** (Finding #22), so it is **not** used. The ML that *does* run is the **regime classifier**
(82.6% OOS) inside the Regime/Technical desk, used as a **capital allocator / risk read**, not a price
predictor. Honest ML: the good model earns its place; the disproven one stays out.

## Supporting inputs (not weighted desks)

- **Mantle Fundamentals** — DeFiLlama chain TVL / fees / mETH staking (free, keyless) → ecosystem
  risk-on/off tilt for MNT.
- **Character Tilt** — profiles each asset (predictable vs flow-following) and tilts the desk ranking.
- **Carry** — delta-neutral funding-carry monitor.

## Reliability weights

The PM doesn't average the desks — it weights them by **learned reliability** (`desk_performance`): each
desk earns a weight `0.6–1.4` from its realized track record (stance vs realized direction, ≥15 samples).
Neutral until proven. See [Self-learning](/docs/self-learning).
